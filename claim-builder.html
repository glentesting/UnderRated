Next steps

You have three ways to submit your claim:

  1. VA.gov — File online at va.gov/disability/file-disability-claim-form-21-526ez. Fastest processing.
  2. Mail — Send VA Form 21-526EZ with supporting documents to your regional VA office.
  3. Work with a VSO — A Veterans Service Organization (DAV, VFW, American Legion) can file on your behalf at no cost.

Tip: If you have a VSO, share this summary with them. It gives them everything they need to file efficiently.
