# UNDERRATED

VA benefits intelligence for veterans. UNDERRATED helps service members understand their VA disability rating, find unclaimed conditions, and build stronger claims — no runaround, no guesswork.

## Features

- **Rating Calculator** — Enter your service-connected conditions and ratings. See the VA's "whole person" math explained step by step, including how far you are from the next compensation tier.
- **Gap Identifier** — AI-powered analysis based on your MOS, service era, and current conditions. Surfaces unclaimed conditions with specific reasons you likely qualify.
- **Vault** — Upload your VA rating decision PDF. Conditions, ratings, and decisions are extracted automatically.

## Tech Stack

- Static HTML/CSS/JS frontend
- Supabase (auth, database, RLS)
- Vercel serverless functions (PDF extraction)

## Disclaimer

UNDERRATED is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We help veterans understand and prepare VA claims — always consult a VSO or accredited attorney for complex situations.
