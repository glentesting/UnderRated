Gap identifier

We analyze your conditions against 38 CFR Part 4 criteria, surface unclaimed secondary conditions, and identify MOS-specific gaps.

Secondary conditions
Conditions caused or worsened by ones you already have. Example: PTSD causing sleep apnea. No new in-service event needed — just a nexus letter.
MOS exposure
Conditions common to your specific military job. Infantry veterans get tinnitus and back injuries. Artillery gets hearing loss. Your MOS tells the story.
Presumptive conditions
Conditions the VA automatically grants based on where/when you served. Vietnam? Agent Orange. Post-9/11? Burn pits. No proof of connection required.
