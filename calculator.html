How VA math works

The VA doesn't add ratings together. It uses the "whole person" method — your highest-rated condition takes its percentage from 100%. Each next condition takes its percentage from what's left, not from 100%.

Painful motion rule (38 CFR § 4.59): If any of your joint conditions cause pain on movement, the VA is required to assign at least the minimum compensable rating (10%) — even if range of motion is near normal. Make sure your C&P exam documents pain, not just ROM.
